Our Origin Story

Where We Started

Overclocked is a computer retail business founded on the values of convenience, accessibility, and excellent customer service. Our journey began with a passion for high-performance components, which we meticulously select and overclock to ensure optimal performance. This site was created for users to buy elite components, assuring them that we only offer the best.

Where We're Going

Today, as we serve you online, our dedication to growth also extends to each and every customer. We strive to provide solutions, reliable services, and affordable but quality technologies that will enhance the lives of our customers and community.
As we move to greater heights, giving you the best customer experience is still our number one goal.
